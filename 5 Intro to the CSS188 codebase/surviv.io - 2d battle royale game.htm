How to Play

Controls

Movement: W, A, S, D

Aim: Mouse

Melee/Shoot: Left-Click

Change Weapons: 1 through 4 or Scroll Wheel

Stow Weapons (Melee Mode): 3 or E

Swap to Previous Weapon: Q

Switch Gun Slots: T or drag gun to other slot

Reload: R

Scope Zoom: Left-Click on Zoom

Pickup/Loot/Revive: F

Use Medical Item: Left-Click on Item or 7 through 0

Drop Item: Right-Click on Item

Cancel Action: X

View Map: M or G

Toggle Minimap: V

Use Team Ping Wheel: Hold C, then hold Right-Click and drag mouse, then release Right-Click

Use Emote Wheel: Hold Right-Click and drag mouse, then release Right-Click

How to Play

The goal of surviv.io is to be the last player standing. You only live once per game - there is no respawn!

2D PUBG

If you've played other battle royale games like PUBG, Fortnite or H1Z1, then you're already halfway there! Think of surviv.io as 2D PUBG (with slightly less desync and more chicken).

Loot and Kill

You'll begin the game with no items other than a simple backpack. Move around the map to find loot: weapons, ammo, scopes, and medical items. Eliminate other players and you can take their loot!

Red = Bad!

Players aren't the only thing that can hurt you. The deadly red zone will move in from the sides of the map and deal increasingly greater damage if you stand in it. Keep an eye on the map and stay safe.

What's New!

Sept. 21, 2018

Desert rain

PARMA supplies have reached critical levels again, but this time, they've also got way too much sand! It's coarse and rough and irritating and it's getting everywhere. For a limited time only, squad mode will once more feature massively increased flare gun spawns.

This time around, you'll have a few new weapons for the final shootout at Ranchito Pollo. The Model 94 lever-action repeating rifle, chambered in .45 ACP, fires a speedy, accurate and decently powerful bullet.

It's companion gun is the Peacemaker revolver, also chambered in .45 ACP. The Peacemaker is deadly accurate when tap-firing, but it's real strength shows when you hold down fire and "fan the hammer." All remaining rounds in the revolver will empty at an insane pace, albeit, with a steep accuracy penalty.

For all the latest leaks and throwback screenshots, please follow us on Instagram!

Sept. 7, 2018

Bridges of Sweatbath County

The ocean has a new best friend: rivers. These bending bodies of water vary in size, and you'll want to make sure you're not caught on the wrong side of one. Find a bridge to ease your crossing or risk wading into the open waters. Rumor has it a PARMA installation exists underneath the widest of these rivers ...

Two new weapons are also at your disposal. First, the M93R pistol. It may be a close relative of the M9, but the M93R shames it's cousin with three-shot bursts fired at a rapid rate. Hold that trigger down and let the 9mm flow through your enemies.

Second up is the M4A1-S assault rifle. This classic carbine is fully suppressed - your enemies won't hear your shots and they'll be hard pressed to see them, too. The 5.56mm M4A1-S can only be found in rare (gold) air drops.

For all the changes in this update, visit the changelog. As always, get the latest surviv memes and discussion (but mostly memes) on our Discord server.

August 13, 2018

Low-key update

Key rebinding has arrived. Bind shoot to Spacebar, reload to Page Up, or for you crazies out there, Interact to E. Certain keys are disabled due to browser conflicts (see the changelog for the full list).

As a bonus option, you can even share your keybinds with other players! Click the "Share" button in the main menu keybind customization screen and you'll get a keybind code, as well as a box for loading codes.

Our next big content push is slated for later this month or early next month. Keep up on the latest through our Discord server.
